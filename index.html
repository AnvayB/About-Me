RippleMatch

My first internship was at a company called RippleMatch, which specializes in finding qualified internship opportunities for students based on their profile. Its algorithm has a matching service where users enter their skills and preferences onto their profile and select which industries or companies they're interested in, and companies associated with RM would specify what qualities they are looking for in potential interns, so if both parties' interests overlapped, a match was made. Basically I was literally doing an internship where I would help other students find internships. It clearly wasn't a techincal internship, but it helped improve my confidence and communication skills when reaching out to students about the program.
